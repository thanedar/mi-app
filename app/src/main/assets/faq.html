CASHGURU FAQ

 

What is CashGuru?

CashGuru is a cool way to earn coins for doing a variety of fun activities such as downloading apps, playing games, inviting friends and more! You can redeem your coins for virtual prizes like coupons, vouchers, mobile top-up’s from our amazing sponsors!

 

How do I earn coins?

You earn coins for doing different activities such as downloading apps, playing games, inviting friends and more!

 

How do I redeem my coins for rewards?

Simply go to the ‘Rewards’ section on the app and you will see all of the available prizes listed and how many coins it takes to redeem those prizes.

 

Can I transfer my rewards to another account?

No, you can’t transfer coins to another account.

 

Can I refer a friend?

By all means, yes! In fact, the more friends you invite, the more coins you can earn. Go to the ‘Share’ section on the app and invite friends via email, Whatsapp, SMS, etc.

 

When I invite friends do I automatically get my coins or do they need to sign up before I get my coins?

When you invite friends you will be given a unique referral code to send off to them via SMS, Email, Whatsapp or Facebook. When your friend registers using your unique code, you will get your coins!

 

Is there a daily limit to how much I can earn?

Nope! Download and play as much as you like and redeem your coins for rewards.

 

How will I receive my rewards?

Depending on your geographical location (United States, Mexico, Brazil, etc) rewards will be sent to you by either email or mobile message.

 

Does CashGuru cost me anything?

Nope! CashGuru is absolutely free to download and play. If you want to redeem coins for prizes we do require that you become a registered user with a valid email.

 

How will CashGuru use my mobile number?

If for instance, you are in Mexico or Brazil, we will use your mobile number to send you mobile messages to redeem airtime as your reward. We do not require a mobile number in the U.S.

 

Is CashGuru available on the iPhone?

No, not at this time.

 

How do I unsubscribe my mobile number and account?

Oh no! We’d hate to see you go. If you must, simply delete the app from your phone and send an email to cashguru@skillsweet.com telling us: “I had a blast, but the party's over. Please delete my account from the CashGuru system. Thank you!”.
